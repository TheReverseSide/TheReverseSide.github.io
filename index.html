Welcome to

My design portfolio

Researcher in the Computer Science department at the University of Helsinki, Finland. Background in psychology and cognitive science with a desire to apply that knowledge to games, particularly serious games. 



Philosophy and background

Why I make games

 Knowing the brain, how it works and where the weak spots are allows you to place challenges exactly where you want to challenge players in specific ways.  Cognitive science, being the study of perception, memory, attention, learning, reasoning and problem solving, is extremely relevant because all of those neurological processes are involved as players are playing the games.
