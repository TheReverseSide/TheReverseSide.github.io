002 : Space Escape

Intelligenzia inaugural game jam 2019. Theme: Sensory deprivation.

The inspiration for this game was a fun limitation of the players sensing capabilities, and try to see how it influences puzzle-solving behavior. This led me to XR, because it seemed like the ideal way to isolate players and therefore control what senses they have and which they do not.

Players initially start with touch only, having to feel the walls for the light switch. Gradually, they unlock and subsequently must combine several of the senses to solve the final puzzle and get the ship working.



Roles

VR developer, Producer, Environmental Design;

I was responsible for doing all of the game development work in VR as well as creating the game environment.
