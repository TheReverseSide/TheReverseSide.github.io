004 : Battle Beetles

Aalto University Game Project 2

Players set out to compete against each other in a multiplayer setting, avoiding environmental hazards and above all, avoid getting knocked off their beetle. If knocked off their beetle, players become extremely vulnerable and open to elimination in this Beetle Royale!

What makes this game interesting is that our team experimented with a second person camera. The player controls the beetle when they are mounted, but the camera follows the beetle. So if the character gets knocked off their beetle, the camera runs away with the beetle!

My roles: Game Designer, UI/UX; I was part of a team that designed and implemented the UI for the game, as well as ran play testing to improve the user experience.

Project details: Built in Unity over 3 months. Team of 6.
