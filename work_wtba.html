006 : Where the Birds Are

Global Game Jam 2019. Theme: Home

Winner of Finnish Game Jam awards 2019!

Our team intended to create a wonderful audio ambient environment that grows in complexity and beauty as the player continues to progress. The visuals were meant to be low-poly complemented by nice lighting and fog effects to give the sense of a deep forest.

The hook: The synchronization of sounds is crucial: the sounds are triggered at the exact moment the birdhouse is placed, and mixes seamlessly with the existing music in the game.



Roles

Game Designer, Environment production, Animations, 3D assets and textures;
for this project I mainly focused on the environment, including implementing the environment in Unity, creating various textures and making simple animations for the birds, among other tasks.


Tools

Unity, Blender, Adobe Photoshop
